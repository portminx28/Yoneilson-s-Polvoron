About Product

Introduction

Yoneilson’s Polvoron is a powdered candy products that offer a sweetness to all types customers. Yoneilson’s Polvoron is a Filipino-style that made of toasted flour, powdered milk, sugar, and butter. It is sweet, and easy to customize with different flavors.

“YONEILSON’S POLVORON: MELTS in YOUR MOUTH, NOT IN YOUR HAND”


OBJECTIVE

To promote healthy competition among other competitors by tagging an equilibrant and just price. Description of the product: Polvoron is a special food that is made up of milk, flour, butter, white sugar, peanuts and cheese.

VISION

Yoneilson Polvorón envisions itself on becoming the top producer of sweet delicacies in Malaybalay City and the leader in the distribution of healthy and enjoyable treat.

MISSION

Our mission is to serve customers great tasting nutritious dessert in today’s highly competitive environment, it is becomes increasingly difficult to compare our product to the usual Polvorón that consumers usually buy. Hundreds of retailers who are selling different pastries are evolving.
